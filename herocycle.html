Intro

In a time of great peril, the people of another world summon you to act as their saviour. Guided by the currents of fate, strengthened by ancient powers, you are to become ever greater, and yet that same fate will inevitably draw new threats to you and leave you scarred irrevocably. The cycle of the hero's journey will repeat, over and over again, until you break the world or it breaks you.

Help

This CYOA is primarily divided into two stages: the Planning stage and the Adventure stage.

In the Planning stage, (the stage you are in right now) you use whatever points you have to purchase options from the Growth, Change, and Damage sections.

In order to end the stage, you must be at or below your Growth point limit, exactly at your Change point limit, and at or above your Damage point limit- you can always take more drawbacks, after all.

Once ready, the Adventure button will light up, allowing you to switch to the Adventure stage. Your selections will be locked in, and a set of potential adventures will be made available. After choosing an adventure, and having your victory or failure determined, you will receive a new set of points and be moved back to the Planning stage.

This cycle will repeat until any Stat reaches a total of 10 and triggers an End. Whether this is a Good or Bad end depends on the particular Stat, so you may wish to be careful!

Note now that while Stats are used to determine success on Adventures and the End you receive, the text of each option is a better descriptor of their actual effects.



You may selectively toggle or scale the effects of Growth options, and specific parts of Growth options, to whatever degree you desire, up to but not exceeding their normal maximum strength. You may expect Growth options, especially those that enhance the same stat, to combine for an ultimately superior effect, even when they might seem contradictory or redundant.

Change options can be resisted or temporarily disabled with some amount of effort. Expect to require a small amount of active effort to avoid slipping back to the 'base state' they set.

Damage options are very difficult to counteract. The more fully you have undone or mitigated their effects, the harder it becomes to weaken them another step. Should you not maintain active efforts to keep them at bay, they will very quickly return to full strength. In places where they directly conflict, absolute statements made by Damage options should override those made by Growth options.

It is possible to acquire new abilities outside of the options you take. The world you were summoned to is filled with magic and advanced yet rather un-industrial technology.

Report

Might

These options primarily enhance the Might stat. Might represents simple, physical power.

Fast

You are extremely fast, capable of acting at up to 60x normal speed. This is entirely selective; you may decide to heal at an enhanced rate while moving and thinking at normal speeds. Negative or unwanted processes, including aging, the progression of biological needs like hunger and breath, the accumulation of stress and boredom, and the progression of poisons and curses can be forced to progress in realtime while you maintain maximum acceleration in other areas, though not slowed beyond this level.

You can diminish the effects and/or consequences of air and water resistance, frictional heat buildup, momentum transfer, relativity, the speed of light, the speed of sound, and the like up to your full acceleration factor, avoiding the standard flaws of rapid movement and allowing you to pull off such feats as carrying a glass of liquid full to the brim at 60 miles an hour without spilling a drop, even when you stop on a dime. Any projectile you launch while accelerated may maintain as much of this effect as you desire, for up to three seconds.

Though this power cannot directly increase the potency of any blow you inflict, nor directly weaken enemy attacks on you, it will never diminish or worsen them.

Faster

Your maximum acceleration factor sees an immediate improvement, rising by a full order of magnitude. You are now capable of increasing your acceleration factor through training and any augmentative effect designed with it in mind (such as a custom spell or precisely-tailored cybernetics). The growth this provides is linear at worst; you will never find the returns of a year's training to be less than the last years, though they may be proportionately less significant when compared to your new maximum.

Given additional efforts, you may develop new abilities of any sort that makes sense as an extension of 'enhanced speed', such as a form of control over vibrations; these abilities scale in power with your maximum acceleration factor.

Any effect that would slow you down against your will, from heavy weights to full-body paralysis to a physical override of your nervous system, will gradually but permanently lose its ability to interfere with your movements and acceleration factor.

Fastest

Your maximum acceleration factor increases again, rising by another full order of magnitude. The rate at which your acceleration factor improves, by all means, is itself increased by an order of magnitude.

Whenever you find yourself in a contest of speed with another entity, you will, at worst, slightly surpass them. You will never find that a foe can move and strike faster than you can see, even for brief moments of maximum exertion, nor that your quarry can outpace you. Effects beyond this that would increase your speed, but not to beyond your opponent's speed, will have no additional benefit; you are already being artificially raised to their level.

The abilities you can develop from your acceleration factor may stray farther from the idea of 'enhanced speed', allowing you to do such strange things as creating lasting bubbles of warped time.

Telekinetic Shroud

You are enveloped in a shroud of telekinetic power, which acts semi-autonomously as best suits your will and desires. Though it has limited capability to outright generate force, it can redirect force very effectively, and though it is normally has very short range, it can spill over physical surfaces you come in contact with. Simple redirections of kinetic energy allow it to improve your grip, disregard issues of leverage, and spread your weight across unstable surfaces that would otherwise collapse.

While not true flight, you can adjust your momentum and rotate your body midair in ways otherwise impossible, and on the ground it is extremely difficult to make you lose your balance. Any atttacks you inflict in melee are much more controlled, and can be made more or less lethal as you wish; you could safely use a sword as a club or prevent the force of your blows from damaging vital organs. Attacks on you are blunted and dispersed to a lesser degree, a portion of the force redirected away from your most vulnerable structures and the overall attack slowed and dispersed as if you had a bit of extra padding.

Kinesis X

Your original telekinetic shroud is no more, though this telekinetic aura retains all of its original capabilities, and more. This aura can outright create and destroy large amounts of force and energy, enabling true flight and much stronger barriers. Range remains limited, and what you can do with the portion of your aura wrapped around another physical object is improved to a notably lesser extent.

The abilities you use this aura to produce are less limited, and can include such things as absorbing and nullifying heat from what it wraps around or generating lasers from any point on your body. Though they are not limited by your understanding of them, and take minimal concentration to use, you similarly cannot develop them through purely mental efforts; repetitive, physical training and experimentation is necessary to develop novel abilities.

The overall power of this telekinetic aura scales with your physical attributes- improved strength, vitality, dexterity, and so on will all improve it substantially. Should you have any other telekinetic abilities, they will empower each other; this aura will gain strength equal to the total strength of your other abilities, and grant new strength to your other abilities that totals to equal its own strength. Additionally, all other telekinetic abilities you possess will be able to function more autonomously, with a diminished need for mental focus and true understanding.

Kinesis Y

Your original telekinetic shroud is no more, though this telekinetic aura retains all of its original capabilities, and more. This aura can outright create and destroy large amounts of force and energy, enabling true flight and the outright levitation of objects. Range swells to well over a hundred meters, and you acquire a limited sense for all things you interact with via telekinetic force.

The precision with which you may manipulate force and energy, combined with the information the aura can grant you, can greatly aid all forms of crafting and manufacturing, given only small amounts of experimentation. Beyond the bare basics, everything you do with this aura requires some degree of mental focus and understanding of the forces involved, albeit much less than it seemingly should. Though you may be able to exert your power over many things at once, there is a limit to how much you can concentrate it; some objects may simply be too dense to lift, and the innate vitality of a warrior may prevent you from just crushing their heart.

The overall power of this telekinetic aura scales with your mental attributes- improved willpower, additional knowledge, superior intellect, and so on will all improve it substantially. Should you have any other telekinetic abilities, they will empower each other; this aura will gain strength equal to the total strength of your other abilities, and grant new strength to your other abilities that totals to equal its own strength. Additionally, all other telekinetic abilities you possess will be able to function much more precisely and controlledly, allowing you to pull off tricks otherwise impossible.

Heroic Form

Your body is augmented extremely thoroughly. Every organ, every bone, every strand of muscle is cleansed of imperfections and enhanced to just beyond human limits. Regardless of your actual physical build, you can easily bench press a metric ton and outpace olympic runners, and with training you can become even greater.

Your biology responds to threats and environmental conditions more intelligently; you will not suffer from any form of allergy, and important parts of your body will never atrophy from simple disuse. Though your healing rate is merely doubled, given time, you can recover from any purely physical wound that does not kill you, up to and including the loss of everything but your brain, perfectly; this effect improves all forms of healing you benefit from.

Unbroken

Your body becomes extraordinary durable. To scrape away a single millimeter of your flesh is as difficult as doing so to twenty millimeters of hardened steel, no matter what form of attack is used. This reinforcement scales with the unaugmented resiliency of every part of your body; your bones are many times tougher than this.

Your sheer vitality is similarly incredible, such that you can survive for well over an hour after having every drop of blood in your body removed, sprint for twelve hours without rest or water, and digest raw cellulose comfortably. Though you are not immune to poison or disease, you are much more resistant, and your body is capable of raising your body temperature by over a dozen degrees without any discomfort, and your cells and organs have access to new ways to process any toxins they detect.

Expect to be able to survive and recover from anything that leaves your brain and the internals of your torso largely intact, as your wounds clot almost instantly and healthier organs take up the slack of ones destroyed in combat. Each time you recover from some form of physical harm, you will become ever so slightly more resistant to it.

Mighty

With sheer physical strength, you can lift, and toss several meters, objects massing well over a hundred tons. Though your durability is enhanced to a much lesser degree, you will never injure yourself by exerting your strength.

This does not diminish the precision with which you can exert or perceive your strength, no matter how much you are currently exerting; if you could previously judge the difference ten pounds of weight makes, you will notice when a ten ton object you are carrying becomes ten pounds heavier.

Should you will it, any object you pick up can be reinforced to a degree corresponding with its size and weight; an ordinary sword will receive much less benefit than one twice as tall as you.

You are not subject to the momentum generated by swinging heavy objects, nor do you need to concern yourself with stable ground becoming less so once you start swinging and lifting things.

Immortal

You are biologically immortal. Age alone will never kill or weaken you, even non-physically, and magic or similar that attempts to manipulate age has no effect on you unless you allow it to. No matter how much time passes, all skills and abilities you possess will remain at their peak, even gradually returning to it if artificially diminished.

Your mind is strengthened to endure the passage of aeons, capable of maintaining a healthy outlook indefinitely and eventually recovering from even the most horrific psychological scars. Farther, so long as even one of your vital organs remains intact, you will not die, and can maintain some minimal degree of function.

Unbreakable

Your simple durability increases by well over an order of magnitude, and your stamina becomes truly infinite. The damage an attack can inflict upon you is solely determined by the raw power behind it; an infinitely sharp blade is no more dangerous to you than a wooden club, and a spell to turn your body into sand is no more dangerous than one that conjures fire. Even should an attack go around this, warping or erasing the medium of your existence, your body produces its own exotic forces; you will be undeterred by the erasure of the four fundamental forces or your own history alike.

Nothing that lacks any form of will or intelligence can cause you true harm- a wolf is more dangerous to you than a supernova. The amount of damage any single attack can do to you is limited to one fifth of the amount necessary to kill you, and you become truly invulnerable for a period of one second after experiencing each such attack. As long as a decent chunk of your brain or brain-analogue remains more-or-less intact, you will survive.

Herculean

Your basic strength increases by a full order of magnitude, though the strength you can truly exert, in more limited situations, rises to truly ludicrous levels. Given time to do so, you can very slowly lift up any single thing, such as a castle or mountain, heft it onto your back, and move at a slow walking speed with it. This object will not be significantly disturbed by the effort, even dishes in cabinets remaining safely in place. Similarly, when in a direct contest of strength with any other being, you are guaranteed to succeed, even, and especially, if they cheat.

Your strength can be applied in situations where it would simply not be valid otherwise, allowing you to punch ghosts, stab hurricanes to death, and fire a twenty-pound bow with a thousand tons of force. While standing on smooth, perfectly flat ground, you could grab hold of a spaceship and prevent it from lifting off.

Magic

These options primarily enhance the Magic stat. Magic represents versatile personal abilities.

White Magic

Your overall supply of magical energy is increased modestly, and you become intuitively able to apply it towards simple white magic. Whenever someone near you is injured, you gain a small portion of magical energy to fill your reserves.

Infusing magic into injuries, you may accelerate the natural healing process greatly and subtly alter the body in ways that mimic and aid surgery. Stopping bleeding and stabilizing patients is trivial, and you can restore a dying man to full health in the space of an hour.

Magic may be applied to fuel the metabolism, effectively allowing you to directly convert it into nutrition and sustenance for yourself and others as necessary. Mundane poison and disease can be eliminated with a simple spell, and you can weave temporary wards around people and places to blunt certain forms of harm. By converting your magic into a soft, mystical light, you can illuminate even the deepest darkness and temporarily dazzle even blind enemies.

Deathbreaker

Your overall supply of magical energy is greatly increased, and your ability to wield white magic expands in the field of combating death. Whenever someone near you dies, you gain a portion of magical energy to fill your reserves, and your natural regeneration is increased slightly by the number of dead people in your general area.

By pouring magic into any part of a corpse, a soul, or an undead of any kind that was once a person, you may perfectly resurrect it. This heals any wounds, undoes any bindings placed on the target after death, and soothes any woes your target may have in regards to leaving the afterlife or dying originally. This takes a very large amount of magical energy, even for someone who died moments ago, and grows linearly more expensive the longer ago your target died, but can be performed over arbitrarily long timeframes, allowing you to bring back that ancient king over the course of a few weeks.

Additionally, you may apply your magic to create, and seize control of, undead. There is no limit to the amount of undead you may control, and they have no upkeep cost, even if they originally did- your vampires no longer need to drink blood, though they still can, and will still reap the full benefits of doing so. The more magical power you pour into creating an undead, the more of its original strength will be retained, and all forms of creation and control grow gradually cheaper the longer your target has been dead, in total.

Cursebreaker

Your overall supply of magical energy is greatly increased, and your ability to wield white magic expands in the field of aiding the living. Whenever someone near you receives a curse, a spiritual wound, or some ailment that would be especially difficult to resolve, you gain a portion of magical energy to fill your reserves, and your natural regeneration is increased slightly by the number of people suffering from such things in your general area.

You can heal any ailment, break any curse, purge any corruption, through a simple application of your magic no more complex to you than drawing breath. Given enough power, you could undo the curse dooming an entire kingdom in the space of a short evening.

A whispered word, carrying a diffuse cloud of magic across a battlefield, can eliminate all trauma the survivors would otherwise have borne. Your magical healing is no longer limited by the natural healing process, allowing you to regrow lost limbs and organs and even mend damaged cybernetics.

Elementalism

Your magic is attuned to the elements fire, air, water, and earth. By studying these elements and attaining a deeper understanding of their physical, magical, and spiritual nature, you will rapidly develop strong, non-conflicting affinities for them. Very quickly, this will greatly increase your overall supply of magical energy, and allow you to draw a significant amount of magical energy from the presence of these elements and related phenomena (such as the sun and moon).

By suffusing a specimen of any of these elements with your power, you may take control of it, moving and reshaping it with your will and taking in information on its current state. With enough power, you may create more of any element, though this is much costlier with heavier substances. As your affinity for an element rises, this will become cheaper and easier, though as you stray from the "natural form" of the element, this will grow costlier and more difficult- pure gold is harder to work than raw stone.

Elemental Forge

Nestled within your affinity for each element is a spark of motive force. Nurturing this force with large amounts of magical energy, you can split off smaller sparks, using them to create loyal elemental beings. Unless you actively, intentionally give them the spark of true intelligence, they will be as automatons, nonsapient and ethically safe for any form of abuse.

These elementals possess their own limited supplies of magical energy, which they will allow you to tap as necessary. You can give your elementals bodies composed of any substance or substances you can manipulate with your elementalist magic, and they will be able to maintain them, even slowly conjuring new material as necessary. The stronger their bodies, and the more physical force they are able to exert with them, the less your elementals can wield their magic to manipulate the world around them. Those anchored in heavy bodies can be dispersed through physical damage alone, but are near-immune to magical attack, while those remaining as diffuse spirits are the opposite.

Elemental Transmutation

Your overall supply of magical energy is greatly increased, and now extends to a deeper yet still-material level. With a minimal expenditure of power, you may convert one element into another, condensing fire into air into water into earth, or dissolving stone into vast firestorms.

As your elemental affinity and understanding of the materials and processes improves, you may perform more precise transmutation, condensing the heat of a firestorm into a gold coin or a sliver of metal into a mesh of delicate wires. Through a complex process of partial transmutation, you may infuse elemental attributes into any material thing- no matter how powerful or complex it already is- without hindering it in any way save removing previous elemental infusions. A shield infused with elemental water might self-repair, flowing and resolidifying like water and ice, while an armor plate infused with air might maintain an effective mass less than a tenth what it should be.

Narrow Paths

While your old affinities remain, you gain new attunement to every concept- from "metal" to "weapons" to "swords" to "this sword" to "the sword blow I plan to deliver at this exact future date". Though in theory you could learn to wield the magic of omnipotence, broader fields require much more study to receive the same benefits and control. As with building elemental affinities, your supply of magical energy will grow as you develop your affinities, and you will find more and more things providing you with additional magical energy.

While you can use this to replicate the effects of other choices, you will find them to be somewhat less efficient, at best, and would benefit from an incredible synergy should you both purchase and study them. As your affinity for each concept grows, your ability to pull off bizarre conceptual feats will correspondingly rise; with enough investment in cutting, you can learn to disregard distance.

Baleful Glow

By meditating on any sort of being, a classification of creature about as broad as undead, demon, or humanoid, you can nurture a baleful light, innately hostile to all of their kind. You can do this any number of times, creating as large an array of such lights as you desire. For each such light, you effectively gain a small reservoir of magical power, which refills when its chosen foes are destroyed and is only useful for effects antagonistic to them.

Any of these lights- though only one for each individual effect- can be worked into any effect- any form of magic, any mundane blow, any item you craft- allowing it to inflict additional harm to your target, weaken their resistances, and take the place of any particular weaknesses they may have, as well as rendering it proof to any attempts to manipulate or control it by your chosen foe short of outright destruction. A simple infusion of a light that hates the undead into an ordinary man can make his blood burn like holy water in a vampire's mouth for several days. The glow of this light is obvious to those it hates, but otherwise quite subtle.

Ritualism

Your mind is filled with a vast library of arcane rituals for literally every occasion. No matter how much or how little time or wealth you have to spare, you will be able to find something tailor-made for your particular needs. Making use of these rituals, you can apply any of your abilities with greater range, power, efficiency, permanency, or so on, or stray farther from normal function than should be possible.

A grand ritual might allow you to resurrect every corpse in the empire as a zombie, while a more modest one might let you charge up an attack for five minutes before the start of battle.

With this, you may bind phenomena into place, be that place an item, location, or creature, causing them to repeat themselves endlessly- forcing a bomb to go off over and over at the center of your reactor, or a sword to continue emanating the fire spell cast upon it years ago.

Aetherflux

One of the extensions of your soul is dotted with seven nubs, each immersed in a chaotic flow of aetheric flux. Given a few minutes, a random, chaotic power will crystallize around any nub left empty. This power will dissolve about an hour later if unused, or after a few minutes of use when the power it contains runs dry.

The precise nature of each power is truly random, but they will never be incredibly potent or versatile on their own, and remain limited by their internal energy reserves. You might get the power to metabolize nitrogen as if it were oxygen, to dissolve rope on touch, or to fill people's mouths with swarming insects as easily as the power to fly via gravity manipulation, fire matter-disintegrating beams, or predict the near future.

No matter how weird they get, no power will ever cause you harm except by your own deliberate will.

Flux Memory

Every time you lose one of your flux derived powers, your soul builds a duplicate from the memory left behind. Unlike the original, this memory will not fade or break down from the passage of time. By channeling your own magical energy through it, you can wield the power again, for as long as you would like.

Unfortunately, these memories are significantly less efficient than the original power, and can only safely channel as much power at a time as the original used. For a brief moment, you can channel any amount of power through a memory, but doing so will destroy it irrevocably.

When created, each memory contains a small reservoir of magical energy, which can be tapped freely, but never restored.

Flux Storm

The powers you crystallize from aetheric flux are significantly stronger, though this power surfaces in what may seem to be bizarre ways. They are substantially less likely to produce generic abilities, such as flight or simple energy attacks. You might find an ability that allows you to forge portals between areas of extreme temperature differential, but disregards distance and the lightspeed delay entirely, or one that lets you teleport yourself into the future to dodge attacks.

The particular powers you acquire are quite often suited very well to your current situation, or to a situation you will face in the near future. Moments before you realize someone unsavory is following you, you might gain the power to recolor cloth, the power to adjust the length of hair, and the power to project a specific human voice from any nearby location.

Artifice

These options primarily enhance the Artifice stat. Artifice represents making use of resources to expand your options and abilities.

Necessary Talent

You possess incredible talent in all things related even vaguely to crafting. No matter how complex the topic, you will be able to understand it fully with a single brief explanation, and your memory, for all subjects, not just those related to crafting, is truly perfect.

You can easily use purely theoretical understanding in place of practical experience, and apply expertise in specialties that should by all rights be useless for your current project. When your abilities are put to task, you will notice every mistake you make, and almost instinctually take the necessary steps to avoid them in the future. In the time it takes you to forge three simple swords, you can easily become a masterful blacksmith.

Necessary Ability

You will never be prevented from completing a project due to some lack of innate ability. You will never be too weak or clumsy to work a forge, nor lack the mental capacity to fully comprehend a design, nor go mad trying to decipher the secrets of alien technology. You could singlehandedly replicate an item that could normally be made only by three trained singers, each bearing an inborn link to a unique form of magical music, or forge enchanted artifacts without the ability to cast a single spell.

Though you are likely to still need some tools, protective equipment of all sorts is much less necessary; you could safely work in a three hundred degree forge with this power alone. This has the greatest effect during the crafting process itself, but you retain some shadow of any abilities granted for crafting even after finishing. There is nothing you cannot reverse-engineer, given time.

Necessary Knowledge

Your ability to singlehandedly push forwards the scientific and technological horizon is comparable to some civilizations. This does not accelerate smaller and more focused projects significantly, but greatly reduces the cost of expanding their scope. Developing every industrial process necessary to mass-produce a new product is only slightly more difficult than developing the new product itself.

You acquire theoretical knowledge at an incredible pace, obtaining many times more information from experiments than should be possible and stumbling across breakthroughs and valuable insights with unsettling regularity. The discoveries you make in this manner are often, but not always, tailored to you current or future needs. You might stumble across the processes necessary to build an arcane forge that can burn ice for warmth and clean water shortly before you are invaded by an army of world-freezing wraiths.

Box of Scraps

The materials, tools, and time you have to work with are less significant limitations on your ability to craft. You suffer only a small fraction of the normal consequences of using cheap materials, working with shoddy tools, or rushing production. Though you cannot make cast iron as strong as whatever alloy you use it to replace, you can make it function much better than it theoretically should. Many smaller problems, such as unpainted iron rusting, cheap seals cracking, and soft drill bits wearing away, will simply fail to occur.

By sacrificing much of the working lifespan of a device, you may allow it to function just as well as a normal version made to the highest standards would, until it inevitably comes apart.

Submaterial Refinement

All materials, components, and tools you use in any form of crafting are treated as if they were vastly better for the task than they actually are. A sheet of iron, hammered into shape by hand and riddled with flaws and impurities, would compare favorably to modern tungsten alloy. Twine soaked in saltwater easily substitutes for copper wire, and nearly-inert wood pulp can replace many magical herbs and reagents. This boost takes effect no matter how high the quality of your tools and materials already is; modern tungsten alloy receiving the same proportionate boost as primitive iron.

Though this cannot grant new properties to your materials, it can greatly magnify them; you could use a tiny pellet of sodium as the core of a decent bomb. Generally, you may expect the precise enhancement a material or tool gains to depend on what would best serve its current intended purpose.

Always Prepared

You constantly discover materials, parts, and equipment on your person, as if, in addition to everything else you do, you spent 24 hours a day working on producing them. This benefits fully from your crafting ability and the resources available to you, yet does not consume anything- if you have a single ingot of a rare arcane alloy, this might gradually give you three swords forged from it, while leaving the original ingot untouched. Indeed, this can build upon itself, slowly accumulating the necessary materials and equipment to create ever-greater products.

Though you cannot control what this produces, the guiding intelligence is precognitive, and will dedicate its efforts towards what you will need most. You might discover a lockpicking device in your pocket after you are imprisoned, or an ice wand on your belt when a fire elemental attacks. When in safe conditions, this will tend towards convenience; you might find a sandwich on your plate at noon, or a replacement to the generator component that just broke down in your toolbelt.

Painless Augmentation

You are extremely capable of applying all of your ability to craft towards the goal of enhancing yourself and others. Given time, you are able to find a way to use anything even vaguely crafting-related to augment someone. You might learn to extend the duration of a potion, even rendering it permanent, to interface human nerves with computer systems by stabbing through them with copper wire, or to engrave runes onto human flesh that simply should not function on such a medium.

No matter how risky a procedure may theoretically be, you will never encounter any complication worse than a simple lack of benefit. The trade-offs normally inherent to such augmentations, including those of necessary maintenance, are reduced or eliminated, and many major problems simply cease to occur. You can trivially install a flintlock gun in someone's arm, rigging it to fire through the palm when they flex a certain muscle, and not cause any health issues, even when smoke seeps out through flesh and the kick of the gun jars muscle.

Unbound Alteration

Your ability to modify any motive entity is almost unlimited. Even without any understanding of surgery, or any tools beyond distilled water and a knife, you could surgically induce the total restructuring of a person's body into any other human form. When you observe the basic underpinnings of a being, be it DNA, a nanobot's structure, or the web of power composing a spirit, you immediately comprehend the function of each part, and what you would need to change to inflict specific changes.

Up to a certain point, modifications you install in motive beings have no form of upkeep cost, drawing additional resources, from energy to nutrients to freshly-made bullets, from the simple fact that you are their origin. When you do establish trade-offs of some kind, the benefits are much greater than may seem reasonable; you could grant biologically immortality and perfected regeneration capable of enduring millions of years by reducing fertility a fraction of a percent.

Simply by installing a device within a living being, it becomes significantly more effective than it would otherwise be- that arm cannon is twice as powerful as cyberware than it would be mounted on a vehicle. The more resources you invest into augmenting a given being, the more capable it will become- though the cost for the same degree of improvement slowly rises, you will never encounter a hard limit.

Mass Proliferation

Your capacity for mass production is expanded. Given time to establish the necessary infrastructure, and access to the necessary resources, you can stamp out mass quantities of products only slightly inferior to the best of what you can make yourself. This entails both teaching apprentices, and ensures they can develop the necessary skills and abilities, and establishing automated systems, ensuring they can function properly with minimal, or even no, oversight. Production lines you establish effectively receive most of the benefits of every ability you possess that would aid in crafting of any sort, even when later expanded by others.

Additionally, given an example of some product of especially high quality, you can derive from it materials useful in the creation of similar but somewhat lesser products. You might drip oil over a legendary sword, using it to lend greater power to new blades as you quench them, or create clones of a mighty being that retain a fraction of even those powers that have zero genetic basis.

Extended Tree

The end of the tech tree is not your limit. You are capable of achieving perfected, truly optimal designs considerably easier than normal- solar panels as efficient as is physically possible, spells that do not waste any energy that can theoretically be retained. With additional efforts, you can then go even farther beyond, developing devices others may consider 'impossible'. Though this alone does not permit anything truly novel, remaining within the realm of simple statistical enhancements, it may make what is otherwise impractical or not cost-effective a reasonable choice. At a certain level of greater-than-maximum efficiency, you might operate a cyclical fusion-fission reactor that splits and recombines atomic nuclei in a process that creates significnat amounts of new energy.

This extends to all things that may be optimized, (though some may require a sequence of tools to build the tools), including technological devices, spells, magical artifacts, biological structures, governmental systems, martial arts, and software.

Deep Extraction

When you set out to find materials, what you find is not limited to what exists. The farther you journey, the more danger you accept, the higher the cost you pay, the greater the rewards you might find. At the lowest level, you might be able to extract an endless supply of high-quality ore from a mine stripped clean long ago, or harvest as much lumber as you need from a forest without cutting down a tree that truly exists. At higher levels, you might hunt down and slay a monster that did not exist before your hunt so that you may use its heart to fuel a forge, dig past the bottom of the world to find jewels and ores only imagined, or fight through deep wilderness to discover a hidden village with inhabitants willing to trade souls for strange elixirs. There is no true limit to what you might obtain.

Outfits and items of clothing completely dissimilar to your chosen style will change uncontrollably towards the same generic expression of it, while outfits and items of clothing made to match in specific ways but stretch the limits in others can partially deny its influence.

Armor and other solid equipment is much less effected, but may have its coloration altered and symbols and tiny decorations (also chosen when this ability is acquired) added.

Entitlement

Choose a title of some kind, as simple as "Lord" or as complex and longwinded as a transcript of a seven-book series of novels. Anyone who sees you will find this title snapping to mind, and know that it identifies you. This title may be used in place of any other form of identification or documentation without issue; it encodes everything necessary to identify you as you currently are.

Unless they have the ability to copy your visage and voice, at minimum, nobody can claim this title for themselves, and no warping will prevent others from recognizing you as its true bearer.

As a fiat-backed effect, every being who you would call your enemy will, on recognizing you, feel, at the very least, the tiniest spark of fear for what you represent- regardless of their capacity for fear, emotion, or self-control.

Aftshadow

Hints and subtle details will pop up in the world around you, referencing events which have not yet occurred. A villain's dying words might pop up in a book you read weeks before first encountering them, you might come across a prophecy you don't understand until everything comes to a close, the name of a great yet wholly unknown monster might pop up on signs in every town you pass through for multiple years before your first battle with it. These details will make the conclusion of any adventure seem more satisfying, to make more sense, as if everything was leading up to this point.

The actual net effect of these details is effectively none; they cannot provide any useful information, nor effect the outcome of anything.

Unseen

These options are defined by a subtlety of manifestation.

Withdrawn

Upon your death, your soul will fold in on itself, disappearing almost completely from the rest of reality. This prevents you from passing on to an afterlife, having your soul captured or consumed, and so on. In this state, you are extremely difficult to detect, and even harder to truly harm. At the same time, this makes it nearly impossible for you to wield any sort of power over the outside world.

Standard forms of resurrection and reanimation- powerful magic, cloning and mindstate backups, undead ascension, temporal revision- take additional effort to perform on you, and are easier to resist.

Damage Dispersion

The damage caused by any physical injury you receive is dispersed across your body. This prevents any one attack from killing you simply by hitting something particularly vital; a strike barely greater than a papercut used to slit a major artery will do almost nothing to you. Even minor wounds, such as repeated stabs to one part of one foot, may eventually kill you. Though natural healing is well-suited to repairing this dispersed damage, very simple healthcare may be somewhat less useful.

Self-Assessment

Through some mechanism, be it an intuitive awareness or a series of menus only visible to you, you have a fine awareness of your current status. This provides precise measures of the powers you possess, your various attributes, the resource pools at your disposal, and even your health and any conditions affecting you.

Checking this assessment takes some amount of concentration, and relying on it to determine your limits makes it somewhat harder to break them.

Honesty

You are no longer able to say(or write) anything that you believe to be completely false. This does not prevent you from lying by omission, manipulating what you believe, applying past voice recordings or writings to a new situation where they are not true, or deceiving others in any way that is not lying outright.

Anyone you encounter will know intuitively that you are unable to speak(or write) an untruth.

Understanding

Any communications you perceive are translated flawlessly to your preferred language, carrying with it enough metadata to grant you a perfect understanding of what was intended to be expressed. Any communications you produce, be it speech, writing, or something stranger, are translated flawlessly in a similar manner to the preferred language of whatever being you have in mind while producing them. This is an extremely subtle effect; you would have a hard time noticing if someone speaking to you switched from one language to another mid-sentence.

Freedom

Your nature, whatever it may be, less strongly defines you. Should you become a new kind of being, the benefits and disadvantages will become less extreme; as an undead, holy water would have less effect on you, yet necromantic energies might be slower to mend your form. Any source of strength you find is not as much a part of you as it otherwise would be, acting more like a tool- at a cost in ease of use, its effects on your true self are diminished, especially when left unused for long periods. The double-edged 'blessings' of an evil god may work a bit more bluntly in your hands, but conversely have a harder time warping your emotions.

Bloodthirst

Every time you kill something real and self-aware, that on some level does not want to die, you become slightly stronger, slightly better. Though mightier foes provide a greater reward, the scaling is not linear; a hundred-meter dragon's life might be worth as much as a few hundred mortals. The majority of this growth is applied to whatever allowed you to kill your foe; giving someone a stroke with precise telekinesis might give you enough physical strength to lift a few more grams, but enough telekinetic strength to lift a few dozen.

This power has a cost: your powers, attributes, and skills will slowly fade away over time, at a rate comparable to what you would gain from killing several mortal men each day. Though this diminishment will never reduce you to below the level of an ordinary mortal, it will continue even if you are locked in stasis. Growth gained while you are suffering from this diminishment will automatically be applied to help you recover from it before becoming new growth; you will recover old powers before raising them to new heights.

Should a being you killed ever be resurrected, any strength you gained from its death will be immediately lost to you.

Motive Flux

Your emotional state and motivation has a significant influence on all of your abilities. If you feel you cannot win, or do not strongly wish to, you will be weaker than normal; if you are confident and know firmly that you must win, you will be significantly stronger. Some mental states can cause abilities to shift in odd ways; it may be easier to magically summon rain while you are experiencing deep sorrow, you will grow stronger more quickly for some time after making a significant personal change.

To a lesser degree, the emotions and motivations of anyone you care about, regardless of their current location and state, can also influence your capabilities. If every member of a kingdom you have grown to love and dedicated yourself to protecting fiercely wishes for one shared goal, then in the pursuit of that goal, for a brief time, you can wield incredible power.

Fateful Exchange

A lesser aspect of the grand fate overseeing this world attaches itself to your existence. It offers fair bargains, bad fortune in exchange for an equivalent amount of good. You gain a reservoir of fate, which you may expend to generate good fortune- perhaps an attack misses you instead of hitting, you discover an extra bit of treasure in that box, or an action you attempt is simply more successful.

So long as this reservoir is not full to the brim, the aspect of fate paired to you has authority to give you bad fortune, filling your reservoir a bit with every moment of foul luck it sends your way. Though admittedly potentially catastrophic, this aspect seeks only entertainment, and aims to use this power to make your life more dramatic and interesting- it will never try to put you in a no-win situation.

Semifertility

Choose a level of fertility, anywhere from 0% to 100%. This is the chance of a pregnancy occuring whenever you have some form of sexual intercourse where at least one participant can theoretically become 'pregnant'. This factor is not impacted by most means of modifying the odds of conception, nor by interspecies incompatibility.

Perfected Digestion

Your body becomes capable of transmuting physical substances and dispersing matter into the interdimensional void. Though these processes are energetically expensive, significantly increasing your raw caloric requirements, you effectively no longer require anything but water and calories and do not produce waste.

Metabolic Shift

Your metabolic requirements for air, water, and food are replaced by a need for a single kind of substance. This could be metal, plant matter, fire, crystal, emotions, or so on. Given you consume enough of this substance, filling your reserves, you could comfortably go well over a month without ingesting any.

You retain the ability to ingest, process, and enjoy normal air, water, and food, but can only slow your hunger at best feeding upon them. This does not reduce the efficacy or impact of anything not standard air, water, or food, such as a potion, drugs, or supernaturally beneficial food.

Move Speed

Consider the set of your ground, air, aquatic, space, tunneling, climbing, and swinging mobility. At this point, you may freely assign your speed and ease of movement in any of these to any other; this could entail applying your ground speed to all others, allowing you to move as quickly and easily through any environment as you could run.

A form of mobility that has been overwritten by another no longer benefits from the normal effects that would enhance it- if you overwrite your air mobility with your aquatic mobility, simple flippers may enhance your flight speed more effectively than a divinely forged ring of flight. This has minimal effect while you are wholly encompassed in a vehicle, though the line between vehicle and armor can be nudged in either direction with effort and will.

Natural Habitat

Your comfortable environmental range, including aspects of temperature, gravity, pressure, and atmospheric composition, can be set to any arbitrary state. Note, however, that this only moderately expands your range of safe environmental conditions; if you are comfortable in the center of a star, you could easily freeze to death in a scorching desert.

New Light

You gain a new sense, flawlessly integrated into your mind, and the additional mental processing capacity to handle it. It can be linked to any part of you- your soul, an eye, your skin or bones- and can be any sort of sense: echolocation or infrared vision is just as valid as passive arcane divination or fine-tuned gravity detection. The raw power of this sense, in terms of range and detail, will always be slightly greater than the best of your other senses, though some may remain more useful under specific circumstances. As your best way to perceive the world, this sense may become as defining to you as sight is in most humans.

Mutation

These options are defined by obvious physical alterations.

Basic Rescale

At this point, you may alter the overall scale of your body. This change is proportionate to your prior size, but is sufficient to render an average adult human anywhere between 3 and 9 feet tall.

You are protected from the normal consequences of a drastic change in size, such as nerve signal propogation rate, biological failures at modified scale, and pitch and volume issues from resized auditory recepters and emitters. Should you grow, your proportional strength will not be reduced by the square cube law, and you will become slightly more dextrous and in control of your body, mitigating most of the difficulty of handling objects of your original scale. Should you shrink, you will similarly lose less strength and durability than you theoretically should, such that objects that were heavy before are only slightly heavier.

Extreme Rescale

At this point, you may alter the overall scale of your body again. Though the change remains proportionate to your original size, unmodified by Rescale options, it is extreme enough to render an average adult human anywhere between 1 foot and 10 meters tall. The protections against the consequences of modified size become more extreme as you approach more extreme sizes.

Ridiculous Rescale

At this point, you may alter the overall scale of your body again. Though the change remains proportionate to your original size, unmodified by Rescale options, it is ridiculous enough to render an average adult human anywhere between 1 inch and 100 meters tall. The protections against the consequences of modified size become more ridiculous as you approach more ridiculous sizes.

Skin Shift

At this point, you may alter the appearance and nature of your skin or skin-analogue. This can alter coloration freely, and add or remove features such as scars, tattoos, moles, and freckles. Your only limitation in this is what is vaguely plausible in a mundane creature; you could render specific sections of your skin completely smooth and hairless, or add scales, fur, chitin, feathers, or so on in whatever varieties and combinations you desire at any locations.

Unusual Skin

At this point, you may alter the appearance and nature of your skin or skin-analogue. Your skin may now resemble anything, and possess more extremely unnatural properties. Living rock, liquid metal, constantly-lubricated latex, and literal barkskin are all valid possibilities.

Decorative

At this point, you may alter the 'cosmetic elements' of your body. This includes, but is not wholly limited to, hair, eyes, ears, horns, teeth, sexual organs, non-prehensile tails, and similar. You may add, remove, rearrange, reshape, recolor, and generally modify these elements without limit. If you replaced your face with a single giant eyeball, taking up all space where a brain could possibly be, you would not suffer any consequences save those naturally stemming from a lack of the other facial features you removed.

Reshaped

At this point, you may alter the basic structure of your body. Though your ability to modify your musculature and fat deposits is unlimited, your capacity to adjust your bones (or bone-analogues) is constrained. While you may reshape them, you cannot add or remove bones, nor alter how they are linked. You may be able to alter the bones in an arm and hand to create the structure of a wing, but not make new wings sprout from your back.

Timelock

At this point, you may alter your apparent age. This is not limited to picking one age and receiving its biological traits; you may choose to have the overly-large eyes of a younger individual while remaining fully developed in other aspects. These traits only modify your appearance, and will not effect your physical ability or lifespan, though similarly, they will not change with the passage of time.

Acquired Instability

You gain a second form. This form is identical to your original body, save that you may apply customizeable Change options to it differently. As an example, if you had Decorative, each form could have completely different cosmetic features. This applies to all options obtained before, after, and simultaneously with this option.

Swapping between forms requires exposure to some specific manner of substance, energy, or supernatural effect, set when this option is acquired. Injuries and negative conditions do not transfer between forms.

Relimb

At this point, you may alter the set of limbs attached to your body. This allows you to add or remove any number of limbs. Valid limbs include such things as prehensile tails, hooved legs, wings, and boneless yet surprisingly strong tentacles.

Destructure

At this point, you may alter your overall bodily structure. This includes, but is not limited to, the modification of your limbs, and encompasses such possibilities as "becoming a tentacle monster", "becoming a centaur", and "becoming a spider".

Recomposed

At this point, you may alter the fundamental substance and underpinnings of your body. This has limited effects on your external appearance; if you become a living suit of armor, you will retain something approximating your original hair. Possible compositions include a slime with restricted transformative abilities, a literal walking tree, an elemental, and so on. Modifications to your internal organs, and the replacement of your brain-analogue with a different manner of structure, are trivial.

Good Touch

At this point, you may alter the set of your erogenous zones. You may scale intensity to any point between "zero effect" and "orgasm on touch". Every part of your body may be adjusted seperately; a specific patch of skin two millimeters across could be set to maximum intensity, while everything else on the entire body part is without sexual feeling. This is not limited to body parts that possess sensory ability.

Outflow

At this point, you may alter your production of sexual fluids. Each specific fluid may be adjusted entirely independently. You may alter taste, consistency, coloration, production rate, emission rate, and storage capacity without limit, and set your refractory period. The production of sexual fluids will never cost your body nutrients, and they will be far easier to clean up.

Bounty

At this point, you may alter how pregnancy works for you. Even if you are incapable of becoming pregnant, this may be used to set how it functions for your sexual partners.

You may make a pregnancy longer, shorter, more pleasant, safer and easier, and so on, to whatever degree you desire. You may additionally apply special factors, such as replacing live birth with the production of eggs, switching to a form of oviposition, or causing eternal, constantly-repeating pregnancies.
